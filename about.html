THE ORIGINS OF PLANIT YOU!

Something about the class, idk.

The origins of Planit You and its subsequent designs came from a discussion on what to name our web application. After bouncing around a few different potential product names, Caelen came up with the idea of “Planit”, as in “Plan It”. Almost simultaneously, we all realized the similarity of the name “Planit” with “planet”, and thus our space-themed web planner was born.
